About me

My name is Luciana Terrazas and I’m originally from Brazil. Currently, I’m a San Francisco based linguist and newbie coder seeking a new career path in Software Engineering. I first came to the United States in 2016, when I was enrolled in a two-year Cultural Exchange Program to learn English. In the summer of 2019, after finishing my undergraduate studies at the University of São Paulo, I've relocated to the US permanently. I would love to chat with you about previous experiences, interests and challenges faced while developing this project. You can get in touch with me at luciana.cterrazas@gmail.com . Thank you!

About this project

As a Latin immigrant I’m aware of how effective communication can often get lost in translation. Knowing that San Francisco is home to a large Latin population, I felt compelled to ensure that language wouldn’t be a barrier for my community to find healthcare, especially during the year of 2020. So I built ¿Hablas Portuñol? - a Python web application that allows users to locate Spanish and Portuguese-speaking health providers in San Francisco. They can also write reviews, bookmark doctors and contribute to my open source data feature. You can check out more about this project here!
